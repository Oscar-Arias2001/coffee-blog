Why is coffee such a sensory experience?

It is enough to hear someone say the word coffee to perceive its aroma, taste it in the mouth and even feel its warmth in the body. But why does this happen?

Coffee is not simply a beverage, it is a ritual. Its sensory experience is due to its composition, but also to hundreds of years of production, harvesting, roasting and preparation techniques.

Surprising from its planting process to the moment you feel it on the palate. Its stimulating effects are able to awaken the senses and to perceive life with a different taste. That is why in this website we propose you to know everything about the world of coffee. And when we say everything, we mean absolutely everything.

We are CoffeeBlog and in this adventure we intend to guide you with different courses about history, customs, flavors and aromas of our favorite drink.

One of the most consumed beverages in the world deserves a space like this and you deserve to discover this world beyond the borders of instant coffee.
