Our Upcoming Courses and Workshops

Building Sensory Skills: One Cup at a Time

Price: Free

Students: 20

In this course, you'll build your ability to identify what you smell and taste in your cup. Then you'll practice describing and sharing your sensory experiences with others. And all throughout, you'll form a deeper appreciation for the beverage you already love.

Origin and Ethical Sourcing

Price: Free

Students: 20

In this course we'll explore the history of coffee, agriculture, varietals and processing methods. We'll discover the differences in growing countries and our steadfast commitment to ethical sourcing. You'll explore what influences a coffee's flavor: growing, processing, roasting and blending.

Roast and Blend

Price: Free

Students: 20

In this course we will explore how master roasters and blenders use different techniques to unearth coffees' flavors. In ways, the art and science of roasting and blending coffee is about perfecting nature. We'll also dive deeper into ethical sourcing, brewing coffee and crafting espresso beverages.
